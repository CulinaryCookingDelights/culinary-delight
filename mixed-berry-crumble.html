Mixed Berry Crumble

A warm and comforting dessert featuring a medley of seasonal berries topped with a crispy, buttery oat crumble. Perfect with a scoop of vanilla ice cream!

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins
Servings: 8
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

For the Berry Filling:

  • 6 cups mixed berries (strawberries, raspberries, blueberries, blackberries)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

For the Crumble Topping:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For Serving:

  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Oven

    Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. Make the Berry Filling

    In a large bowl, gently toss berries with sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, vanilla extract, and salt. Transfer to the prepared baking dish.

  3. Prepare the Crumble

    In a medium bowl, combine flour, oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Add cold butter cubes and use your fingers to work the butter into the dry ingredients until crumbly.

  4. Assemble and Bake

    Sprinkle the crumble topping evenly over the berry mixture. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until the topping is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

  5. Serve

    Let cool for 15 minutes. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and garnish with fresh mint if desired.

Chef's Tips

  • Use fresh berries when in season, but frozen berries work well too (no need to thaw).
  • Keep the butter cold until ready to use for the best crumble texture.
  • Place the baking dish on a sheet pan to catch any bubble-over.
  • Store leftovers covered in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.
