Lemon Garlic Chicken

A bright and flavorful chicken dish that combines the zesty tang of lemon with the rich, aromatic essence of garlic. Perfect for a delightful weeknight dinner.

Main Course Poultry Easy
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Serves: 4
Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 lemons (1 juiced, 1 sliced)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Remove chicken from refrigerator 15-20 minutes before cooking. Pat dry with paper towels and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Make the Marinade: In a bowl, combine minced garlic, lemon juice, 2 tablespoons olive oil, thyme, and rosemary.
  3. Preheat the Pan: Heat remaining olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering.
  4. Cook the Chicken: Place chicken in the hot pan. Cook for 5-7 minutes per side until golden brown and internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  5. Create the Sauce: Remove chicken from pan. Add butter and remaining marinade. Add chicken broth and lemon slices. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Finish and Serve: Return chicken to pan, coat with sauce, and garnish with fresh parsley. Let rest for 3-5 minutes before serving.

Chef's Tips

  • For even cooking, pound chicken breasts to an uniform thickness of about ¾ inch.
  • Use fresh lemons and garlic for the most vibrant flavor.
  • Don't overcrowd the pan to ensure proper browning and caramelization.
  • Check internal temperature with a meat thermometer:
    • Chicken is safe at 165°F
    • Let rest to allow juices to redistribute
  • Serve with rice, roasted vegetables, or a light salad to complement the dish.
