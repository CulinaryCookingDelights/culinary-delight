Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl

30 mins Easy 2-4 servings
Vegetarian Quick

A vibrant and nutritious bowl packed with Mediterranean flavors, featuring protein-rich quinoa, fresh vegetables, and a zesty herb dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • Lemon-herb dressing

Instructions

  1. Rinse quinoa thoroughly. Combine with water in a pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes until water is absorbed.
  3. Fluff quinoa with a fork and let cool slightly.
  4. Chop cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and herbs.
  5. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, vegetables, olives, and feta.
  6. Drizzle with lemon-herb dressing and toss gently.
  7. Garnish with additional herbs and serve.

Nutrition Facts

Calories 350
Protein 15g
Carbohydrates 45g
Fat 12g

Chef's Tips

  • Toast quinoa before cooking for a nuttier flavor.
  • Use fresh herbs for the best taste.
  • Can be served warm or cold.
