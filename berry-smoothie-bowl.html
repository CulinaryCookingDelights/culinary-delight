Berry Smoothie Bowl

Breakfast Healthy Vegan

Start your day with this vibrant and nutritious berry smoothie bowl. Packed with antioxidants and topped with fresh fruits and superfoods, it's a delicious way to energize your morning.

Prep: 10 mins
Serves: 1
Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • Toppings:
  • Fresh berries
  • Sliced banana
  • Granola
  • Coconut flakes
  • Hemp seeds

Instructions

  1. Add frozen berries, banana, and almond milk to a high-speed blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more milk if needed to achieve desired consistency.
  3. Add honey or maple syrup if desired and blend briefly to combine.
  4. Pour into a bowl.
  5. Top with fresh berries, sliced banana, granola, coconut flakes, and hemp seeds.
  6. Serve immediately while cold and enjoy!

Chef's Tips

  • Use frozen fruit for a thicker smoothie bowl consistency
  • Add protein powder or Greek yogurt for extra protein
  • Prepare toppings before blending to serve immediately
  • Experiment with different fruits and toppings for variety
