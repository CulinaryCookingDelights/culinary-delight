Creamy Spinach Artichoke Dip

A rich and creamy dip loaded with spinach, artichokes, and melted cheese. Perfect for parties and gatherings, this crowd-pleasing appetizer is sure to disappear quickly!

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins
Servings: 10
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 (10 oz) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 2 (14 oz) cans artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Tortilla chips, crackers, or bread for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients

    Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Squeeze excess moisture from thawed spinach. Chop artichoke hearts into small pieces.

  2. Mix the Base

    In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, mayonnaise, and sour cream until smooth. Add minced garlic, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.

  3. Add Vegetables and Cheese

    Stir in drained spinach, chopped artichokes, Parmesan cheese, and 3/4 cup of the mozzarella cheese until well combined.

  4. Bake

    Transfer mixture to a 9x13 inch baking dish. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese. Bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and lightly browned on top.

  5. Serve

    Let cool for 5-10 minutes before serving. Serve warm with tortilla chips, crackers, or bread.

Chef's Tips

  • Make sure to thoroughly drain both the spinach and artichokes to prevent a watery dip.
  • Allow cream cheese to come to room temperature for easier mixing.
  • Can be prepared up to 24 hours in advance and baked just before serving.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.
