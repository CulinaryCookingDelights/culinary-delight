Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free plan?

The free plan includes access to basic recipes, community features, and our newsletter. You'll be able to browse and try out our platform before deciding to upgrade.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

How do the cooking classes work?

Premium members get access to live and recorded cooking classes led by professional chefs. Classes are scheduled monthly and recordings are available in your account.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial of our Premium plan so you can experience all the features before committing.
