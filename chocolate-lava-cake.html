Decadent Chocolate Lava Cake

Dessert Chocolate Baking Special Occasion

Experience pure chocolate bliss with our Decadent Chocolate Lava Cake. Each bite reveals a perfectly baked exterior giving way to a warm, molten chocolate center that flows like liquid gold. Garnished with fresh berries and a touch of mint, this dessert is the perfect finale to any special meal.

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 12 mins
Serves: 4
Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 4 oz (113g) high-quality dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup (113g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (120g) powdered sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • Fresh raspberries and blueberries for garnish
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish
  • Additional powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Butter and lightly flour four 6 oz ramekins.
  2. In a double boiler or microwave, melt chocolate and butter until smooth.
  3. Whisk in powdered sugar until well blended.
  4. Add whole eggs and egg yolks, then vanilla. Stir until well combined.
  5. Stir in flour and salt until just mixed.
  6. Divide batter among prepared ramekins.
  7. Place ramekins on a baking sheet and bake for 12-14 minutes until edges are firm but centers are soft.
  8. Let stand for 1 minute. Invert onto serving plates.
  9. Garnish with fresh berries, mint leaves, and dust with powdered sugar.
  10. Serve immediately while centers are warm and flowing.

Chef's Tips

  • Use room temperature eggs for better incorporation
  • Don't overbake - the center should be soft and gooey
  • Prepare ramekins in advance for smooth workflow
  • Best served immediately while the center is warm
  • For perfect unmolding, run a knife around the edges before inverting
